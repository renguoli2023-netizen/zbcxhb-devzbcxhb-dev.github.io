Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Video Upload Assistant application ("the Service"), operated by zbcxhb.net ("the Company"), collects, uses, and protects information. This application is an internal tool for authorized employees and does not collect or process data from public TikTok users.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Employee Account Information

  • Employee login credentials (managed internally)
  • Authentication tokens for our company's TikTok account

2.2 Content Information

  • Videos scheduled for upload
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags)
  • Upload schedules and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain our video upload service
  • Programmatically upload videos to our company's TikTok account
  • Manage our internal content schedule
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

4. Third-Party Platform Integration

TikTok API Usage: Our application uses the TikTok API to upload videos to our designated company account. We do not access, collect, or store any data from other TikTok users. Our use of the API is limited to content posting functions for our business account.

5. Information Sharing

As this is an internal tool for our company's use, we do not share any information with external third parties, except for the video content that is uploaded to TikTok as part of the service's core function.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect our internal data, including:

  • Access controls for authorized employees only
  • Secure storage of API credentials
  • Regular security assessments

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
